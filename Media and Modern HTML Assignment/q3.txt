Assistive devices are technologies or tools that help individuals with disabilities access and interact with digital content and physical environments. These devices play a significant role in providing accessibility by compensating for impairments and enabling equal participation. 

Web accessibility is essential because the internet has become a fundamental part of modern life, offering access to information, services, and opportunities.

 some common types of assistive devices used to enhance accessibility:-

1.  Screen Readers
2.  Screen Magnifiers
3.  Braille Displays
4.  Alternative Keyboards
5.  Switch Access Devices
6.  Speech Recognition Software
7.  Closed Captioning
8.  Eye Tracking Devices
9.  Reading Pens
10. Assistive Listening Systems
